Contribute

If you like Tatool and want to support its cause, there are multiple options to do so. We're very happy about any kinds of contributions!

Code-Sharing

As the Tatool platform is all about sharing code with fellow researchers, we welcome any contributions in the form of Tatool components (e.g., executables, stimuli files or stimuli in general, modules, etc.). To share your mnaterials and the necessary documentation, please complete this form. Also, if you have ideas about how to improve or extend the Tatool Web platform, please just contact us via our Github page!

Donations

Hosting of Tatool unfortunately isn't for free, and involves quite a few technologies. No matter how small, any donations via PayPal are very welcome and much appreciated, and will allows us to keep Tatool up and running:

  • Web Hosting (Server & Database)
  • Domain Name
  • SSL Certificate & Hosting (we'd love to get one with your support!)
