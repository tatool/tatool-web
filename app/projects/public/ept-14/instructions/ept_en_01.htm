Instructions

The purpose of this test is to see how you solve small tasks that may come up in everyday life.

You will be shown documents (graphics, tables or texts) that you might come across in your everyday life such as labels, credit applications or bus schedules.

Each document is accompanied by a multiple choice question about it.
Read the question first. After working out the answer, press the number on your keyboard corresponding to the correct answer.
You do not necessarily need to read through the entire document to answer the question.

IMPORTANT:

You must answer these questions WITHOUT help from anyone else.
You must NOT use any extra materials like a calculator.
Please GUESS if you are not sure how to respond.
There is only ONE correct answer per question.
Answer the question as if YOU were using the real document.
You have 1:30 minutes to answer each question.

When you are ready, press the right arrow key to get started with an example question.
